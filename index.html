How to Use Every Morning
1

Open at 8:00 AM

Tap the OptionEdge icon on your home screen. Best window is 8:00–8:30 AM — US markets have closed, Gift Nifty is live, and FII/DII data is fresh.

⏰ Set a daily 8 AM alarm labelled "OptionEdge"
2

Check Your Settings

Verify capital (₹2,00,000), risk (Medium), instrument (Nifty+BankNifty), expiry (Weekly) are correct. Change only when needed — e.g. switch to Monthly in expiry week.

3

Tap "Analyze Today's Market"

Runs 5 AI calls with live web search. Takes 60–120 seconds. Each section loads one by one: Macro → News → Strategy → Trades → Option Chain.

📶 Needs internet — use WiFi or mobile data
4

Read Macro Snapshot

Check Gift Nifty, US market close, India VIX, FII/DII. These show overnight global mood. Green = tailwind, Red = headwind.

🔑 VIX above 16 = use spreads, not naked options
5

Read Strategy Outlook

Shows today's bias (Bullish / Bearish / Sideways), key support-resistance levels, and which option strategies suit the day's conditions.

📌 Screenshot this for quick reference during trading
6

Check Chain Tab → PCR & OI

PCR above 1.2 = bullish, below 0.8 = bearish. Highest CE OI = resistance. Highest PE OI = support. ATM row highlighted in blue.

📊 Max Pain strike is where market tends to close on expiry
7

Pick 1–2 Trades from Trades Tab

6 ideas are shown. Don't trade all 6. Pick 1–2 that match today's bias from Strategy. Check entry, target, SL, and capital required.

✅ Verify the strike exists on NSE option chain before ordering
8

Size Your Position in Calc Tab

Enter the trade's premium and your stop loss % into the Calculator. It tells you exactly how many lots to trade without overrisking your capital.

🧮 Never risk more than 2% per trade when starting out
9

Place Orders by 9:10 AM

NSE opens 9:15 AM. Have Zerodha / Groww / Upstox ready with order details filled in. Use limit orders, not market orders.

⚡ Wait till 9:30 AM if first 15 min looks very volatile
Quick Glossary
CECall Option — profit if market goes UP
PEPut Option — profit if market goes DOWN
ATMAt The Money — strike near current price
OTMOut of The Money — cheaper, lower probability
OIOpen Interest — no. of active contracts
PCRPut-Call Ratio — >1.2 bullish, <0.8 bearish
VIXVolatility Index — >16 means high fear/volatility
Max PainStrike where most options expire worthless
FII / DIIForeign / Domestic big institutional investors
Iron CondorNeutral strategy, profit if market stays flat
Bull Call SpreadBullish, limited risk and reward
Bear Put SpreadBearish, limited risk and reward
SLStop Loss — your maximum acceptable loss exit
Lot SizeNifty=75, BankNifty=30, FinNifty=40 units
